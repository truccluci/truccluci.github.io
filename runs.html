Concrete

Runs calculator for making sand.

4 Inputs: Trunk Type, Tycoon Premium, Post-Op Perk, # of Concrete Wanted.

Inputs
1. Pickup: Raw Gas
2. Raw Gas → Waste Water
3. Waste Water → Sulfur
4. Chemicals → Acid
5. Pickup: Unfiltered Water
6. Unfiltered Water + Acid → Treated Water
7. Pickup: Crude Oil
8. Treated Water + Crude Oil → Kerosene
9. Kerosene + Sulfur → Explosive
10. Raw Ore Mix + Explosive → Sand

Features

Calculates runs for 3 different trunk types, with/without Tycoon Premium, and with/without the Post-Op Perk active.

Assumes 10 processes for obtaining sand using the ROM-Explosive method. The process of obtaining ROMs is not included. The processes are numbered as a rough flow of events, not a strict flow.

Treated Water runs are for the Diluted Fuel Refining process to make kerosene. Does not take into account water needed for making concrete.
